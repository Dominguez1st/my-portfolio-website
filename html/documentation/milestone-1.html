Purpose, Audience, and Goal

  • Purpose: This project is to be a professional portfolio website with which to demonstrate my full stack development skills
  • Audience: This site is to be designed for a professional audience, and is made to target individuals within the web development community. Target audience to include: hiring managers, recruiters, and peers
  • Goal: The primary goal of this site is for users of the site initiate contact with me in regards to employment. The secondary goal of this site is to showcase my full stack development skills, and to contribute to a polished professional online presence.

Persona

  • Name: Steve Johnson
  • Age: 36
  • Profession: Manager of ExcogitatorisWebsites.com, a company that is looking to hire a full-time, in-house full stack developer.
  • Technology: Windows user. Uses a custom built desktop with Windows 10, a HP Pavilion laptop, and a Samsung S8 phone.
  • Attitudes and Behaviors: Fast when skimming but if something catches his eye then he will give a more detailed look at things. Steve uses LinkedIn and other such sites to help narrow down his search.
  • Frustrations and Needs: Steve wants to hire someone to help his website development company grow and expand. Steve wants someone that can work full stack and also knows how to work with a team. Portfolio websites are a good way to see just a person brings to the table but Steve is frustrated that viewing such sites can take a while. Because of this, Steve has begun to skim the sites rather quickly and will only look more in-depth if the site appears to show what he wants quickly, easily, and in high quality.
  • Goals: Steve's goal is to view various portfolios quickly and gauge whether a person is a potential candidate based on what he sees. Steve will quickly view a portfolio and if at first glance the site looks to be of a higher quality then Steve will look at the portfolio more closely. After viewing detail Steve will decide if the candidate is worth contacting to continue the job application process.
  • User Story: As a user, I would view a clean and easy to read portfolio website to see if this person is a candidate for out full stack developer position.
